How it works

From sheet to solid, in three steps.

01 · COMPUTER VISION · SEMANTIC SEGMENTATION

Segment

A vision model reads the blueprint the way a drafter would — identifying walls, doors, windows and fixtures directly on the sheet.

02 · RASTER → VECTOR

Vectorize

The raster sheet becomes clean, layered 2D geometry: true lines and arcs with real coordinates, not pixels.

03 · PARAMETRIC SOLIDS · OPENCASCADE KERNEL

Extrude

A parametric solid-modeling kernel lifts the plan into an editable 3D model — geometry you can revise, not a frozen mesh.

04 · STL / STEP · HUMAN SIGN-OFF

Export & review

Planos flags low-confidence geometry for review instead of pretending to be perfect. The model ships when your reviewer signs off — not before.
